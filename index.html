SINGLE USE SUCKS

Alright, here's
the thing
about plastic...

Plastic was designed to last forever. That’s useful when it holds your food — but not when it leaks into nature.

Every bag, bottle, and wrapper we’ve ever made is still out there somewhere. Instead of breaking down, plastic just breaks up into smaller and smaller pieces that spread through oceans, animals, and even us.

How bad () is it actually?

11 mil.
tons

That’s how much plastic waste ends up in the ocean every year because it isn’t properly handled. Picture a truck full of plastic being dumped into the sea every single minute. This isn’t just waste – it’s a crisis for our planet.

1 min

10-13%

That’s how much CO2 plastic alone is responsible for every year because of its production and disposal. If we want to reach our climate goals by 2050, we need to drastically reduce our CO2 emissions, as experts identify plastic as one of the biggest contributors to the climate crisis.

490 mil.
tons

That’s how much plastic was produced each year (2019), with 50% used for packaging and single-use products. This amount is expected to triple by 2050, potentially accounting for nearly 50% of global oil demand.
